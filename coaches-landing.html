How we work together

Simple, direct — no complicated onboarding

01

We have a conversation about your practice

Tell me who your clients are, what you're already doing, and what you'd like the tool to handle. I'll be honest about what it can and can't do well for your specific use case.

02

I configure the tool for your client types

Based on our conversation, I adapt the setup — industry focus, tone, seniority calibration, output format. This takes a week or two depending on complexity.

03

You start using it with clients, I support the tool

You integrate it into your sessions however makes sense for your practice. I stay available for changes, improvements, and anything that isn't working as expected.

04

We refine together over time

As you use it with more clients, we'll learn what works. Feedback from your practice feeds into how the tool develops. The arrangement stays useful, not static.
