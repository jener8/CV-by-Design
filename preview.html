The process

Strategy first. Tool second. You decide when it's ready.

Step 01

We start with the role, not the document

In a coaching session we clarify what you actually want, who you're targeting, and what your CV needs to communicate. The tool makes building fast — this session makes it strategic.

Step 02

The AI tool builds a strong first draft

Access a purpose-built AI resume builder, configured and adapted to your profile and needs. Within minutes you have a structured, polished document — tailored to you, not a generic template.

Step 03

We refine it together

You review, I give feedback. We adjust tone, emphasis, and positioning until it reflects you accurately and compellingly. This is where human judgement matters most.

Ongoing

Iterate as your search evolves

Applying to different roles or sectors? The tool lets you generate tailored versions quickly. Coaching stays available for the questions, pivots, and decisions that come up.

About the AI tool

Honest about what it is and how it works

The CV tool is AI-powered, purpose-built, and still evolving. I want to be upfront about that. It was built through an iterative, hands-on process — shaped by real client needs, real job searches, and direct feedback on what actually helps versus what just sounds impressive.

That means it gets better over time. New client profiles, new industries, and new use cases all inform how it is configured and improved. It is not a finished product — it is a working tool that I actively develop and adapt alongside the people who use it.

I use it myself. I test it. I update it. And I pair it with coaching because the tool alone, like all AI, works best when a thoughtful human is directing it.

Transparent about AI involvement

The tool uses AI to generate resume content. I don't present this as magic — it is a guided, structured process. You are always in control of what goes into the final document.

Actively maintained and improving

The tool is iterated based on what I learn from each client. Different industries, seniority levels, and search strategies all feed back into how it is configured and refined.

Your data stays yours

Everything you put into the tool is private and secure. It is never shared, used to train external models, or accessible to anyone other than you and me during our work together.

Human judgement is always part of the process

AI writes fast but doesn't understand your career the way you do. Coaching ensures the output is genuinely yours — accurate, compelling, and not just plausible-sounding text.
