What's included

A complete service — not just a tool handed over and forgotten

01

Discovery and scoping

We start by understanding how you actually work — your processes, bottlenecks, and goals. This shapes what gets built and ensures the tool solves a real problem, not an assumed one.

02

Personalised tool design and build

I design and build the AI tool around your specific needs — configured to your workflow, your language, and your outputs. Not a template. Not a plugin. Something that fits.

03

Coaching on how to use it well

AI tools only deliver value when people know how to direct them. Coaching is included so you get the most from what's been built — and can adapt it as your work evolves.

04

Ongoing maintenance and iteration

The tool evolves as your needs do. I maintain it, update it when the underlying AI changes, and refine it based on how it's actually being used. You don't have to manage it yourself.

How it works

A straightforward process from first conversation to working tool

01

Initial conversation

Tell me what you're trying to solve. We'll talk through your work, your pain points, and what a useful tool would actually look like. No commitment at this stage — just clarity.

02

Scoping and proposal

I put together a clear proposal — what I'll build, how long it will take, and what it will cost. Transparent and specific, so you know exactly what you're agreeing to.

03

Build and iterate together

I build the tool in stages, with your input throughout. You test it with real work. We refine until it genuinely fits how you operate.

04

Handover, coaching and ongoing support

You get the tool, the coaching to use it well, and ongoing support to keep it working as your needs change. It doesn't end at delivery.
