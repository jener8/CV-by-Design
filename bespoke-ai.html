How it works

Built with you, not handed to you

01

We start with your actual problem

A scoping conversation to understand your workflow, where time is being lost, and what a tool needs to do to genuinely help — not just look impressive.

02

I design and build a working prototype

Not a proposal document — an actual working tool you can test within the first engagement. We iterate from something real, not from slides.

03

We refine it to fit how you work

The tool is adapted based on how you actually use it. This is where most AI tool projects fail — I stay involved until it genuinely works for you.

04

Ongoing maintenance and coaching

AI tools need care as models change and your needs evolve. I maintain what I build, and coaching stays available as your use cases grow.
